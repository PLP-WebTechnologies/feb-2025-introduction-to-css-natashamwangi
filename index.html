Girls Go Geek Limited is an organization that designs tailored solutions to make various life experiences, such as pregnancy, easier. Through innovative technology, the organization ensures that expectant mothers have access to vital data throughout their pregnancy, nearby clinics, and guidance on how men can be actively involved in supporting their partners.
Save Mom and Baby is one such solution, designed to provide a seamless and informative journey for expecting parents.

Some of the features include:

  1. Tracker
  2. Google Maps Integration shwoing nearby clinics
  3. Tips on how spouses/partner can help support through the pregnancy period

Tracking your pregnancy journey is an exciting and essential part of ensuring a healthy experience for both the mother and baby. A pregnancy tracker helps expectant parents stay informed about their baby’s development, important milestones, and necessary health check-ups throughout the trimesters.

Features of a Pregnancy Tracker

  1. Development - Provides insights into fetal growth and changes happening in the mother’s body.
  2. Health & Wellness Tips - Offers guidance on nutrition, exercise, and mental well-being.
  3. Appointment Reminders - Keeps track of doctor visits, ultrasounds, and prenatal tests.
  4. Kick Counter & Contraction Timer - Helps monitor baby's movements and prepare for labor.
  5. Checklist & To-Do Lists - Includes hospital bag checklists, baby shopping lists, and birth plan templates.
  6. Community Support & Resources - Connects parents with expert advice and peer support groups.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up & Enter Your Details - Provide estimated due date, symptoms, and preferences.
  2. Track Weekly Changes - Get updates on your baby's growth and maternal health.
  3. Find Nearby Clinics - Locate trusted medical centers for prenatal care.
  4. Engage Your Partner - Share educational materials and tips on active involvement.
  5. Stay Informed - Access articles, videos, and Q&A forums with health experts.
